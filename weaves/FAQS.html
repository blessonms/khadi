Purchase



WHICH PRODUCTS CAN I FIND AT YOUR ONLINE SHOP? +

At Weave of traditions.com you can find a wide selection of products from our catalog for women, men and children, as well as special editions and products made exclusively for the online store. We update our catalog regularly, so come back often!

WHERE CAN I FIND INFORMATION ABOUT PRODUCTS AND SIZES? +

For each product you will find a detailed description, composition and useful information for washing and care of the garment. To help you choose the right size, we have created a Size Guide with conversion tables from the Italian size, shown on the tag and on the product sheet, as well as conversions for different countries.

HOW CAN I CHECK THE STATUS OF MY ORDER?+

You can check the progress of your order at any time on the Order Status page: just enter the email with which you made the purchase and the order number. If you are a registered customer, you can also check your order from your account: log in and enter the 'Orders' section to find all the information. At the time of shipment we will send you an email with the tracking number and the link to the courier's website, where you can see the delivery information.

CAN I CHANGE OR CANCEL AN ORDER?+

It is not possible to modify or cancel orders placed online. After the delivery of the order you can request the return of the products you wish to return and you will receive a full refund of the price of the products. If you find that you have made a mistake in the shipping address please contact our Customer Service as soon as possible.

HOW CAN I SHOP ONLINE?+

It's easy: add all the products you like to your cart, from the catalog or from your Wishlist. While shopping you can always see the contents of your cart, alongside the total value of your purchase. You can change the quantity, size and color of the items you intend to purchase at any time. Before completing the purchase, we will ask you for the delivery address and the payment method you prefer to use. By clicking on 'Buy now' you will confirm your order and we will immediately send you an email with a summary of your purchase. Registered users will also be able to find order details in their account.

DO I HAVE TO REGISTER TO COMPLETE THE ONLINE PURCHASE?+

You do not need to be registered to shop on Weave Of Traditions: you can complete your purchase by simply entering your email (to receive notifications about your order) and a shipping address. However, if you choose to register, you will be able to take advantage of some benefits. Registered users can store their addresses within the account and check the status of their order directly in the reserved area. Register now.

HOW DO 'DISCOUNT CODES' WORK? +

Discount Codes' are vouchers that you can use for special promotions. All the conditions for using the discount codes will be explained together with the code itself. To take advantage of the discount code on weave of traditions.com, enter it in the appropriate field of your cart before completing your purchase. The discount will be calculated and displayed immediately.

WHY WAS MY PAYMENT REFUSED? +

There are several possible reasons. The credit or debit card you have chosen may have expired. You may not have entered all the details of the chosen payment method correctly. Carefully check the data entered and try again to make the payment. If the problem persists, we suggest choosing an alternative payment method or try contacting your bank.

CAN I REQUEST AN INVOICE FOR MY ORDER? +

To receive an invoice for your order, please contact our Customer Service. We will send you the requested document as soon as possible.

Shipping



WHERE DOES YOUR ONLINE SHOP SHIP TO?+

Remember to select the Country where you want your order delivered: the items in your shopping bag and Wishlist will no longer be available if you change Country while shopping.



WHERE DOES YOUR ONLINE SHOP SHIP TO?+

You can choose between different shipping methods at checkout, according to the delivery Country: see all the available options on the Shipping page. If you choose payment through Wire Transfer, please consider that shipping times start from the day on which the payment is received by our bank (this can be up to 3 days after you send the wire transfer).

WHAT HAPPENS IF NO-ONE IS AVAILABLE AT DELIVERY?+

As the courier will request a signature, it's important to choose an address where you or someone else will be available during office hours (for example at a concierge or office address). If no-one is available to receive the order, the courier will leave a note with a contact number. If you need any help organising the delivery, please don't hesitate to contact our Customer Care.

HOW CAN I CHECK THE STATUS OF MY ORDER?+

You can check the status of your order at any time on the Order Status page: simply enter the email address you used for the purchase and your order number. If you are a registered customer, you can also check your order from your account: log in and enter the 'Orders' section to get all the information. When your order is shipped, we will send you an email with the tracking number and the link to the courier's website, where you can see the delivery information.

Returns and refunds



HOW DO I REQUEST A RETURN? +

You have 14 days to request a return if you prefer not to keep the items you've purchased, whatever the reason. Requesting a return is simple and always free: you will find all information printed inside your order box, and also on this page: Easy and free returns.



WHEN AND HOW WILL I RECEIVE MY REFUND?+

All returned items will be refunded through the payment method you chose for your order. The refund will correspond to the final price you paid for the item. We are committed to ensuring your refund is processed as quickly as possible. Once the products arrive at our warehouse, we will take up to a maximum of 14 working days to check that all return conditions have been met. Accepted returns will be refunded shortly after. We will keep you updated throughout this process by email. Please consider the time necessary for the credit to show on your account may vary according to banking procedures and to the payment method you chose.



WILL I RECEIVE A REFUND FOR ANY DUTIES AND IMPORT FEES I PAID AT DELIVERY?+

We're sorry, but returns for orders placed online cannot be delivered to our physical stores. Remember the online return procedure is really quick, and totally free: read more in our Easy and free returns section.
